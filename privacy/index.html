Privacy Policy

NerveReset · Effective date: April 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the NerveReset iOS application (“NerveReset”, “the app”, “we”, “us”) handles your information. NerveReset is designed to be a local-only app: your data stays on your device.

1. Summary

  • NerveReset does not collect, transmit, or sell personal data.
  • NerveReset does not use analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking SDKs.
  • All information you enter in the app (quiz answers, mood ratings, session history, streak, display name, preferences) is stored only on your device using Apple’s SwiftData framework.
  • Subscription payments are processed by Apple through the App Store using StoreKit. We do not see your payment details.

2. Information stored on your device

The following information is stored locally in the app’s private sandbox on your iPhone or iPad and is never transmitted to us or to any third party:

  • Answers you provide to the in-app quiz and the resulting score tier.
  • Pre- and post-session mood ratings you choose.
  • Completed exercise sessions (exercise name, duration, date).
  • Daily check-in streak and related progress counters.
  • A display name, if you choose to enter one during onboarding.
  • Preferences such as sound-cue on/off and preferred reminder time.
  • A local flag indicating whether your subscription entitlement is active.

You can delete all of this information at any time by deleting the app from your device. Uninstalling the app removes the SwiftData store together with every setting listed above.

3. Information we do not collect

NerveReset does not collect any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or physical address (unless you voluntarily contact us by email — see Section 8).
  • Your precise or approximate location.
  • Your contacts, photos, microphone, camera, health data, or motion data.
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA). The app does not present the App Tracking Transparency prompt because it does not track.
  • Analytics, crash reports, device fingerprints, or behavioural data.

4. Subscriptions and payments

Subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store using the StoreKit framework. Apple handles your payment information, billing, and renewals under Apple’s own privacy policy, available at apple.com/legal/privacy.

The app receives from Apple only an anonymous entitlement signal indicating whether an active subscription exists. This signal is kept on your device to unlock premium features. We do not receive your Apple ID, name, email, or payment method.

You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time in Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions on your Apple device.

5. System APIs used

In line with Apple’s privacy manifest requirements, NerveReset declares the following in its bundled PrivacyInfo.xcprivacy file:

  • Tracking: disabled.
  • Tracking domains: none.
  • Collected data types: none.
  • UserDefaults API access: declared with reason CA92.1, i.e. to read and write the app’s own settings (for example the sound-cue toggle). No other system APIs requiring a reason are used.

6. Children’s privacy

NerveReset is not directed at children under 13. The app is rated 12+ on the App Store. Because no personal data is collected or transmitted, the app does not knowingly collect information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided information to us, please contact us at the address in Section 8. As the app stores nothing off-device, simply deleting the app removes any information that may have been entered.

7. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete personal data held about you (for example under the EU GDPR or the California CCPA). Because NerveReset holds no personal data on its servers — everything lives on your device — these rights are exercised directly within the app or by uninstalling it:

  • Access / export: the data you have entered is visible inside the app (Progress tab, check-in history).
  • Delete: delete the app from your device, or use iOS Settings → General → iPhone Storage → NerveReset → Delete App.

If you still wish to submit a formal request, you can write to the address in Section 8. Because we do not store a copy of your data, our response will typically confirm that we hold no information about you.

10. Not medical advice
